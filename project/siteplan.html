Website Plan: M-design Portfolio

Site Name

Name: M-design - Software Development Portfolio

M-design is my professional brand as a software developer. It represents the creative and technical work I do in building websites and applications. The name makes the portfolio feel more like a business presence while still showcasing my own skills and projects.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to present M-design's work, skills, and services in a professional way. It will serve as an online space for potential clients, employers, and collaborators to:

  • View my completed projects
  • Learn more about my areas of expertise
  • Contact me for freelance work or job opportunities

This site will reflect my personal style while positioning M-design as a capable, reliable software development brand.

Scenarios

  • "I'm looking for a developer to build a small web app. What kind of projects has M-design completed before?"
  • "I need a way to contact M-design quickly for a project proposal. Is there a form or email I can use?"

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #1E3A8A (Dark Blue) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and important highlights.

Accent Color: #FBBF24 (Amber Yellow) - Used for buttons, call-to-action elements, and hover effects.

Neutral Color: #F9FAFB (Light Gray) - Used for backgrounds and body text contrast.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Poppins - Clean and modern look for headings and titles.
  • Body Font: Open Sans - Easy to read for paragraphs and descriptions.
